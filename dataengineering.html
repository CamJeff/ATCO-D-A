Data Engineering at ATCO

What is Data Engineering?

Data Engineering is the behind-the-scenes work that makes data useful. It involves designing systems to collect, clean, move, and store large amounts of data so that it’s ready for analysis. Think of it like building roads and pipelines that safely deliver data to the people who need it.

Data Engineering at ATCO

At ATCO, our data engineering team builds and maintains the infrastructure that powers analytics across the company. They create secure, reliable pipelines that bring together data from different business areas like energy, logistics, and finance — making sure it’s accurate, organized, and easy to access.

These systems run behind the dashboards, reports, and tools that employees use every day. Whether it’s preparing data for a Power BI dashboard or integrating new systems, the data engineering team makes sure everything works smoothly in the background.

Why It Matters

Without strong data engineering, it’s hard to trust or even use data. Clean and well-organized data enables better insights, faster decisions, and fewer errors. By making data more accessible and dependable, our data engineers empower everyone at ATCO to work more efficiently and with confidence.
