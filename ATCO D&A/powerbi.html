Power BI at ATCO

What is Power BI?

Power BI is a powerful data visualization and reporting tool created by Microsoft. It allows users to turn complex data into easy-to-understand charts, dashboards, and reports. Instead of looking through spreadsheets or databases, Power BI makes it possible to explore and understand information using interactive visuals.

How It's Used at ATCO

At ATCO, Power BI helps teams across the company make informed decisions. Whether it's monitoring energy usage, tracking performance metrics, or summarizing financial data, Power BI lets departments view and share insights in real time. Benefits for Employees

Power BI makes it easy for employees at all levels to stay informed. Even without a technical background, users can view dashboards tailored to their role, spot trends, and take action faster. By reducing the time spent searching for data and increasing visibility across departments, Power BI empowers everyone to contribute to smarter decision-making.
